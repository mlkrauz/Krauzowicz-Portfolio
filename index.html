About Me


I'm Mark Krauzowicz. Welcome to the first, simple iteration of my portfolio website!
My hobbies and interests often go hand-in-hand with my professional career. I have been building and running plastic 3d printers for about 7 years now, and I run two metal printers for my day-job.
Other interests include

  • CAD software
  • Space & Physics
  • Blockchain technology
  • Mild gaming

I have had entirely too many programming-101 courses over the course of my education, and the addition of this Fullstack Development course is exactly the type challenge I've been looking for.
I will be revisiting this site and overhauling it over the course of the program. For now, you may find links to my existing github repos!
